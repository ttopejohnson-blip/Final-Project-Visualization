Design Process and Sketches

Research Questions

  1. What factors drive high-value spenders in mobile games?
  2. How does spending differ across game genres and countries?
  3. What is the relationship between session count and purchase amount?

Initial Sketches

[Bar chart sketch]
Genres on X-axis, Average spend on Y-axis

[Horizontal bar chart sketch]
Country names on left, bars extending right

Version 2: Geographic analysis.

Final Design Decisions

  • Four charts to answer different aspects of research questions
  • Color coding for spending segments (Whale/Dolphin/Minnow)
  • Tooltips show exact values for precise reading

Design Rationale

ChartPurposeWhy

Key Insights

Finding 1: RPG and Strategy games show the highest average spend per user.
Finding 2: There is a strong correlation between session count and total purchase amount.
