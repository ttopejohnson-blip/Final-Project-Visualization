Research Questions

  • What factors drive high-value spenders in mobile games?
  • How does spending differ across game genres and countries?
  • What is the relationship between session count and purchase amount?

Key Insights

Insight 1: RPG and Strategy genres consistently lead in average purchase value compared to Puzzle or Action titles.
Insight 2: There is a positive correlation between engagement (session count) and total spending, particularly within the "Whale" segment.
Insight 3: Geographic spending is concentrated in high-GDP regions, with specialized trends appearing in mobile-first markets.
