Design Process and Sketches

Research Questions

  1. What factors drive high-value spenders in mobile games?
  2. How does spending differ across game genres and countries?
  3. What is the relationship between session count and purchase amount?

Final Design Decisions

  • Four charts to answer different aspects of the research questions
  • Filters allow users to explore specific subsets
  • Color coding for spending segments (red/orange/green)
  • Tooltips show detailed data on hover

Design Rationale

Chart Type Selections

Chart Purpose Why
Genre Bar Chart Compare average spend by genre Bars make category comparison easy
Segment Bar Chart Show Whale/Dolphin/Minnow differences Clear visual hierarchy of spending levels
Horizontal Country Chart Top 10 countries by total spend Horizontal orientation fits longer names
Scatter Plot Sessions vs purchase amount Shows correlation and outliers

Color Choices

  • Whale Red - draws attention to high-value segment
  • Dolphin Orange - mid-tier visibility
  • Minnow Green - low intensity for lower spenders
  • Genre colors - different colors help distinguish categories

Interactive Features

  • Filters: Genre, spending level, country, device - let users explore subsets
  • Tooltips: Show exact values
